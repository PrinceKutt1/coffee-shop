about us

what makes our coffee special?

We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee. The Wells Coffee is the only place you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind and body.

You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at The Wells Coffee Shop!
